# Referee Reference Sheet

A one-page summary of the procedures used most often at the table.

## X-in-6 Roll

Roll 1d6. Succeed if the result is **≤ X**. A **6 always fails** and a **1 always succeeds**. Modifiers: **±1** (sometimes ±2). Target never falls below 1 or rises above 5.

| Target | % Chance | Likelihood |
| ------ | :------- | ---------- |
| 1-in-6 | 16.7%    | Highly unlikely |
| 2-in-6 | 33.3%    | Probably not |
| 3-in-6 | 50.0%    | Maybe |
| 4-in-6 | 66.7%    | Probably |
| 5-in-6 | 83.3%    | Most likely |

**Ability check** (no defined skill): base **2-in-6** + relevant ability modifier.

## Combat Round

1. **Declarations** — Spells and Disengages.
2. **Initiative** — each side rolls 1d6; higher wins. Ties: simultaneous.
3. **Winning side**: Movement → Ranged → Spells → Melee.
4. **Losing side**: same order.
5. **Morale** — referee checks if applicable.
6. **New round**.

**Attack roll**: 1d20 + AB + STR (melee) or DEX (ranged) ≥ AC = hit.

**Out-of-class weapon**: −2 attack.

## Encumbrance

| Condition  | Items Carried          | Effect                     |
| ---------- | ---------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Normal     | 1 to (10 + STR mod)    | Full movement              |
| Encumbered | up to (15 + STR mod)   | Halved movement            |
| Overloaded | (16 + STR mod) or more | Cannot move or cast spells |

Worn armor: 0 items. Shield: 1 item. Coins: 1 item per 300.

## Movement

| Condition    | Dungeon turn | Combat round |
| ------------ | ------------ | ------------ |
| Unencumbered | 120'         | 40'          |
| Encumbered   | 60'          | 20'          |

Wilderness: 24 miles/day on foot, modified by terrain.

## Dungeon Turn (10 min)

- Move OR take an action per turn.
- Wandering monster check: every **2 turns**, 1d6 → encounter on **1**.
- Failed Break / forced Lock: immediate wandering check.
- Torch: 6 turns. Lantern: 24 turns per oil flask.
- Without light: movement halves; sight-dependent tasks fail; attacks −2; wandering check 1-in-6 per turn; Search/Listen −2.

## Encounter Sequence

1. **Distance** — Dungeon 2d6 × 10' · Wilderness 4d6 × 10 yards (1d4 × 10 if surprised).
2. **Surprise** — 1d6, surprises on **1–2**. Light-carrier in darkness usually cannot surprise.
3. **Reaction** — 2d6 + CHA mod.
4. **Resolve** — negotiate, flee, fight, part ways.

### Reaction Roll (2d6)

| 2d6        | Reaction                                              |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| 2 or less  | **Hostile** — attacks or flees in panic               |
| 3–5        | **Unfriendly** — suspicious; may attack if provoked   |
| 6–8        | **Neutral** — cautious; will negotiate or wait        |
| 9–11       | **Friendly** — open to interaction; may help if asked |
| 12+        | **Enthusiastic** — actively helpful                   |

## Morale & Loyalty (2d6)

Check **morale** at: first casualty · half strength lost · leader falls · unexpected threat.

Roll 2d6. ≤ Morale: holds. > Morale: breaks (flee, surrender, or cower).

Mindless undead, golems, and constructs never check.

PCs are never subject to morale.

**Loyalty**: retainer's personal morale. Adjust over play: +1 for generosity, +1 for life saved, −1 for poor treatment, −2 for carelessness. ≤ 3: retainer leaves.

## Saving Throws

| Category      | Covers                                       |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Death**     | Death effects, poison, instant kills         |
| **Wands**     | Wand and ray attacks, targeted magical beams |
| **Paralysis** | Hold, paralysis, petrification               |
| **Breath**    | Breath weapons, area explosions, blasts      |
| **Spells**    | Spells, staves, rods, magical devices        |

Roll X-in-6 against the character's save value for the category.

| ------ | --- |
| ½–1    | +0  |
| 2      | +1  |

| 4      | +3  |

Monsters save as a Fighter at their HD. **NA** = dungeon encounter (lair).

## Wilderness Day

- 24 miles/day on foot · terrain multipliers (×1 to ×¼).
- Encounter check: **1d6 → 1** twice per day (travel + night watch).
- Getting lost: 1d6 at end of day — 1 (clear), 1–2 (difficult), 1–3 (extreme).
- Forced march (>8 hours): **−1 to all rolls** until full rest.

## Gold for XP

1 gp returned to civilization = 1 XP. Gold left in the dungeon does not count.
