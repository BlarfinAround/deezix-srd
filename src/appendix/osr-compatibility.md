# OSR Compatibility

Deezix runs classic adventures with little adaptation. Four areas differ enough to affect play.

## Saving Throws

Classic saves roll high on a d20, advancing per class and reaching 85–95% on strong saves by high level. Deezix rolls low on a d6 across three level bands, each class having one weak, two standard, and one strong category.

| Level band | Weak | Standard | Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–4 | 1-in-6 (17%) | 2-in-6 (33%) | 3-in-6 (50%) |
| 5–9 | 2-in-6 (33%) | 3-in-6 (50%) | 4-in-6 (67%) |
| 10–15 | 3-in-6 (50%) | 4-in-6 (67%) | 5-in-6 (83%) |

Deezix caps at 5-in-6 (83%); a classic Fighter at high level reaches 65–85% depending on save type, with a higher floor on weak saves. Save bonuses hit harder here: +1 shifts ~17% on a d6 versus ~5% on a d20, making modifiers from spells and equipment more impactful.

## Armor Class and Attacks

Descending AC and THAC0/attack tables are replaced by ascending AC and class attack bonuses. Attack bonus progression is smoother.

| Descending AC | Ascending AC |
|---|---|
| 9 (unarmored) | 10 |
| 7 (leather) | 12 |
| 5 (chain) | 14 |
| 3 (plate) | 16 |

Subtract any descending AC from 19 to convert.

## Skills

Classic adventuring checks are flat 1-in-6 or 2-in-6. Deezix starts at 1-in-6 but adds a relevant ability bonus to one skill per class in lieu of the experience point bonus granted for a primary ability score found in similar games.

Thief percentile skills convert to Skill Points on a d6; concentrated points reach 5-in-6 early but cap there, short of the 95%+ a high-level classic thief achieves.

High-stat characters outperform classic equivalents. Parties find and hear things more reliably. Ensure the players specify the specific areas they are searching and track turns accordingly for skill use.

## Classes

| | Classic | Deezix |
|---|---|---|
| **Fighter** | No special ability | Cleave: free strike on kill |
| **Cleric** | Spells from level 2; plate armor | Spells from level 1; no plate |
| **Magic-User** | Reading or detecting magic costs a spell slot; No magic item identification | Arcane Lore gives a chance to read scrolls/spellbooks, detect magic, and identify magic items and creatures |
| **Thief** | Percentile skills; Backstab damage multiplier | Assignable skill points; Backstab weapon dice multiplier |


## Net Effect

A Deezix party may be stronger than a classic party of the same level. Expect less reliance on hirelings and quicker exploration pace.

Limit access to magic items that grant a bonus to saving throws, or adjust to only adjust one save category.
