# Domains

From around 9th level, a character may carve out a **domain** — a holding that draws followers and earns income between adventures.

## Strongholds

A character who clears a region of monsters and builds a stronghold attracts followers and revenue.

| Class      | Stronghold                        | Followers                          |
| ---------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Fighter    | Castle or keep                    | A company of soldiers              |
| Cleric     | Temple or shrine                  | Devoted faithful and guards        |
| Magic-User | Tower with library and laboratory | Apprentices and guards             |
| Thief      | Guild hall or hideout             | A network of thieves and informants |

Construction costs tens of thousands of gold and takes months; the referee sets exact figures to the setting. A finished stronghold yields income from the surrounding land — taxes, tithes, or trade — paid out between sessions.

## Followers

On raising a stronghold, a character attracts followers drawn by their reputation: soldiers, acolytes, apprentices, or thieves. Followers stay loyal so long as they are led, paid, and housed.

## Downtime

High-level play unfolds largely between adventures — building, ruling, researching, training. Run downtime at a 1:1 scale of real days to game days where possible, so a character spending weeks on construction or magical research stays in step with the party.
