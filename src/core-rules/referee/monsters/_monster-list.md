## Bandit
*Humans who prey on travelers and weak communities.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8, **AL** Neutral or Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 1d8 (3d10), **TT** U (A)

- **Trickery**: Bandits rely on disguises, false flags, and feigned distress to catch their prey unaware.
- **Captain**: A wilderness band may be led by a 2nd-level (or higher) fighter, thief, or magic-user.

## Basilisk
*Squat eight-legged reptile whose gaze turns flesh to stone.*

**AC** 15, **HD** 6+1** (28hp), **AB** +5, **Att** 1 × bite (1d10 + Petrification) + Gaze, **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D4 W3 P4 B3 S2, **ML** 9, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 950, **NA** 1d6 (1d6), **TT** F

- **Petrifying Gaze**: A creature meeting the basilisk's eyes makes a **Paralysis** save or turns to stone. Attackers who avert their eyes fight at −4 and may strike at a reflection instead.
- **Petrifying Bite**: A bite that lands also forces the **Paralysis** save against petrification.

## Black Pudding
*A vast, creeping mass of corrosive black sludge.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 10* (45hp), **AB** +7, **Att** 1 × touch (3d8 acid), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D5 W4 P5 B4 S3, **ML** 12, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 1,600, **NA** 1 (0), **TT** —

- **Corrosive**: Dissolves wood, leather, and metal on contact, destroying ordinary weapons and armor.
- **Splitting**: Weapon and lightning attacks split it into 1d4+1 lesser puddings. Only fire harms it.
- **Wall-Crawler**: Moves at full speed across walls and ceilings.

## Carrion Crawler
*A pale, many-legged worm the length of a horse, its head ringed with tentacles.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 3+1* (15hp), **AB** +2, **Att** 8 × tentacle (Paralysis), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 9, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 75, **NA** 1d3 (1d3), **TT** B

- **Paralysing Tentacles**: Each tentacle that hits forces a **Paralysis** save or the victim is locked rigid for 2d4 turns.
- **Wall-Crawler**: Moves at full speed across walls and ceilings.

## Chimera
*A monstrous hybrid — lion's body, goat's head, and a fire-breathing dragon's neck.*

**AC** 15, **HD** 9** (40hp), **AB** +6, **Att** 2 × claw (1d3) + 1 × gore (2d4) + 1 × bite (2d4) + 1 × dragon bite (3d4) + Breath, **MV** 120' (40') (Fly 180' (60')), **SV** D4 W3 P4 B3 S2, **ML** 9, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 2,300, **NA** 1d2 (1d4), **TT** F

- **Fiery Breath**: Three times per day the dragon head breathes a 50' cone of fire for **3d6 damage** (**Breath** save halves).

## Cloud Giant
*A towering giant, pale and proud, dwelling above the clouds.*

**AC** 15, **HD** 12+3 (57hp), **AB** +8, **Att** 1 × club (6d6) or 1 × boulder, **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D5 W4 P5 B4 S3, **ML** 10, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 1,100, **NA** 1d2 (1d3), **TT** E + 5,000 gp

- **Keen Nose**: Smells living intruders nearby; rarely surprised.
- **Boulder Toss**: Hurls rocks to 200' for **3d6 damage**.

## Cockatrice

**AC** 13, **HD** 5** (22hp), **AB** +4, **Att** 1 × bite (1d6 + Petrification), **MV** 90' (30') (Fly 180' (60')), **SV** D4 W3 P4 B3 S2, **ML** 7, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 425, **NA** 1d4 (1d8), **TT** D

## Cultist
*Devoted servants of dark powers, willing to die for their cause.*

**AC** 15, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6) or 1 × ritual dagger (1d4), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 1d8 (1d20), **TT** U (A)

- **Ritual vestments**: Layered ceremonial robes and blessed bracers function as chain and shield.
- **High priest**: Any band of four or more is led by a cleric of 2nd–5th level (referee's choice of prepared spells).

## Dire Wolf
*A massive, shaggy predator, half again the size of a common wolf, with jaws that can crack bone.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 4+1 (21hp), **AB** +3, **Att** 1 × bite (2d4), **MV** 180' (60'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8 (9 in packs), **AL** Neutral, **XP** 125, **NA** 1d4 (2d4), **TT** —

- **Pack Courage**: A pack of four or more fights at morale 9. The bonus is lost when the pack drops below half its starting count.
- **War-Trained**: Some dire wolves are trained as mounts; a rider uses the wolf's movement and gains the mounted combat bonus.

## Doppelganger
*A faceless shapeshifter that takes the form of those it kills.*

**AC** 14, **HD** 4* (18hp), **AB** +3, **Att** 1 × blow (1d12) or by assumed form, **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 10, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 125, **NA** 1d6 (1d6), **TT** E

- **Shapechange**: Assumes the exact form of any humanoid it has seen, fooling all but the most careful inspection.
- **Mind-Shielded**: Immune to charm and sleep.

## Dragon
*Vast winged reptiles, proud and deadly, each breathing its element.*

Stats below are for an **adult**. Halve hit points for a young dragon; increase them by half for an ancient one.

| Type  | AC | HD   | AB | Attacks (2 claws / bite) | Breath               | XP    |
| ----- | -- | ---- | -- | ------------------------ | -------------------- | ----- |
| White | 16 | 6**  | +5 | 1d4 / 2d8                | Cold (cone)          | 725   |
| Black | 17 | 7**  | +5 | 1d4+1 / 2d10             | Acid (line)          | 1,250 |
| Green | 18 | 8**  | +6 | 1d6 / 3d8                | Chlorine gas (cloud) | 1,750 |
| Blue  | 19 | 9**  | +6 | 1d6+1 / 3d10             | Lightning (line)     | 2,300 |
| Red   | 20 | 10** | +7 | 1d8 / 4d8                | Fire (cone)          | 2,300 |
| Gold  | 21 | 11** | +7 | 2d4 / 6d6                | Fire (cone) or gas   | 2,700 |

**Att** 2 × claw + 1 × bite (see table) or Breath, **MV** 90' (30') (Fly 240' (80')), **SV** as Fighter of equal HD, **ML** 8–10, **AL** by type, **NA** 1d4 (1d4), **TT** H

- **Breath Weapon**: Three times per day, instead of attacking, the dragon breathes its element — a 90' × 30' cone or a 50' × 5' line. Damage equals the dragon's **current hit points**; a **Breath** save halves it. Every creature in the area is struck.
- **Sleeping**: A dragon met in its lair is often asleep (referee's discretion), allowing the party to act or flee before it wakes.
- **Spellcasting**: Older dragons of some types cast arcane spells as a Magic-User.
- **Subdual**: A dragon struck only with the flat of weapons may be beaten into submission rather than slain, and forced to serve for a time.

## Elemental
*A raw spirit of air, earth, fire, or water, called from its native plane.*

Summoned elementals come in three strengths, by the means of conjuring: a staff (**8 HD**), the *Conjure Elemental* spell (**12 HD**), or a great device (**16 HD**).

| HD   | AC | AB  | Strike | XP    |
| ---- | -- | --- | ------ | ----- |
| 8*   | 17 | +6  | 1d8    | 1,200 |
| 12*  | 19 | +8  | 2d8    | 1,900 |
| 16*  | 21 | +10 | 3d8    | 2,300 |

**Att** 1 × strike, **MV** by type, **SV** as Fighter of equal HD, **ML** 10, **AL** Neutral, **NA** 1 (1), **TT** —

- **Elemental Nature**: Harmed only by magical weapons.
- **Air**: Flies 360' (120'); may become a whirlwind that sweeps away creatures under 2 HD.
- **Earth**: Burrows through soil and stone; cannot cross open water.
- **Fire**: Ignites flammables; cannot cross water.
- **Water**: Only within 60' of a large body of water.

## Goblin
*Small, cowardly, and numerous. Goblins are a constant dungeon presence.*

- **Ancient Enmity**: Will attack dwarves — or their setting equivalent — on sight, ignoring other targets if able.

## Gray Slime

- **Elemental Resilience**: Fire and cold attacks have no effect.

## Vampire
*An undead noble of terrible power, draining life and bending minds.*

**AC** 17, **HD** 8** (36hp), **AB** +6, **Att** 1 × touch (1d10 + Energy Drain), **MV** 120' (40') (Fly 180' (60')), **SV** D4 W3 P4 B3 S2, **ML** 11, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 1,750, **NA** 1d4 (1d6), **TT** F

- **Banes**: Cannot cross running water unaided, recoils from garlic and holy symbols, and is destroyed by sunlight or a stake through the heart while it rests.
