## Bandit
*Humans who prey on travelers and weak communities.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8, **AL** Neutral or Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 1d8 (3d10), **TT** U (A)

- **Trickery**: Bandits rely on disguises, false flags, and feigned distress to catch their prey unaware.
- **Captain**: A wilderness band may be led by a 2nd-level (or higher) fighter, thief, or magic-user.

## Cultist
*Devoted servants of dark powers, willing to die for their cause.*

**AC** 17, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6) or 1 × ritual dagger (1d4), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 1d8 (1d20)

- **Ritual vestments**: Heavy ceremonial robes conceal plate-grade protections gifted by their dark patron.
- **High priest**: Any band of four or more is led by a cleric of 2nd–5th level (referee's choice of prepared spells).

## Ghoul
*Undead cannibals that feast on corpses and living flesh.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 2* (9hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 2 × claw (1d3) + 1 × bite (1d3) + Paralysis, **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 9, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 25, **NA** 1d6 (2d8), **TT** B

- **Paralysis**: Any claw or bite that lands forces a **Paralysis** save. On failure the victim is locked rigid for 2d4 turns; elves are immune, and creatures larger than an ogre shrug the effect off. A ghoul will leave a paralyzed victim and pursue another target.
- **Undead**: Soundless until they strike. Immune to poison and to charm, hold, and sleep magic.

## Giant Bat
*Massive cave-dwelling bats, easily as large as a dog.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 2 (9hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × bite (1d4), **MV** 30' (10') (Fly 180' (60')), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 20, **NA** 1d10 (1d10), **TT** —

- **Echolocation**: Sight-based blindness and illusions have no effect. Magical silence, however, leaves the bat blind.

## Giant Centipede
*Multi-legged arthropods 2–3 feet long, fast and aggressive.*

**AC** 10, **HD** ½* (2hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × bite (Poison), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 6, **NA** 2d4 (1d8), **TT** —

- **Sickening Venom**: The bite deals no hit-point damage, but the victim makes a **Death** save or falls violently ill for 10 days — bedridden, capable of only half-speed movement, unfit for any adventuring activity.

## Giant Rat
*Oversized sewer and dungeon rats, as large as a cat, aggressive and numerous.*

**AC** 12, **HD** ½ (2hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × bite (1d3), **MV** 120' (40') (Swim 60' (20')), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 5, **NA** 3d6 (3d10), **TT** C

- **Filthy Bite**: 1-in-20 bites transmit disease. On failed **Death** save, there is a 1-in-4 chance the infection kills within 1d6 days; otherwise the victim is bedridden for one month.
- **Fire-Shy**: Will not approach open flame unless driven by a master.

## Giant Spider
*Ambush predator the size of a large dog, perched above its hunting ground.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 2* (9hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × bite (1d8 + Poison), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 25, **NA** 1d4 (1d4), **TT** U

- **Drop Ambush**: Surprises on a 1–4 in 6 when striking from above or from cover.
- **Wall-Crawler**: Moves at full speed across walls and ceilings.
- **Lethal Venom**: A bitten victim dies in 1d4 turns. **Death** save at +2 to avoid.

## Gnoll
*Hyena-headed humanoids, brutal pack hunters and slavers.*

**AC** 14, **HD** 2 (9hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × weapon (2d4 or by weapon +1), **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 20, **NA** 1d6 (3d6), **TT** D

- **Pack Leader**: Any band of 20 or more is led by a 3 HD gnoll (16 hp).

## Goblin
*Small, cowardly, and numerous. Goblins are a constant dungeon presence.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 1−1 (3hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7 (9 near their king), **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 5, **NA** 2d4 (6d10), **TT** R (C)

- **Infravision**: 90'.
- **Daylight Weakness**: −1 to attack rolls in direct sunlight.
- **Dire Wolf Cavalry**: 20% chance that one quarter of a group rides war-trained dire wolves.
- **Dwarf Enmity**: Will attack dwarves on sight, ignoring other targets if able.
- **Royal Court**: A lair holds a 3 HD king (15 hp, +1 damage) and 2d6 bodyguards (2 HD). Neither suffers the daylight penalty.

## Gray Slime
*Formless ooze that corrodes and consumes.*

**AC** 11, **HD** 3* (14hp), **AB** +2, **Att** 1 × touch (2d8 acid), **MV** 10' (3'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 50, **NA** 1 (1), **TT** —

- **Stone Camouflage**: Nearly impossible to spot against wet stone; surprises on a 1–4 in 6 in such terrain.
- **Clinging Acid**: A successful hit means the slime adheres. It deals 2d8 acid per round automatically, destroying ordinary armor immediately and magical armor within a turn.
- **Elemental Resilience**: Fire and cold attacks have no effect.

## Harpy
*Hideous winged women with a magical, irresistible song.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 3* (14hp), **AB** +2, **Att** 2 × claw (1d4) + 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon) + Hypnotic Song, **MV** 60' (20') (Fly 150' (50')), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 7, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 50, **NA** 1d6 (2d4), **TT** C

- **Hypnotic Song**: All who can hear it make a **Spells** save or are charmed. Charmed targets move toward the harpies, will not attack or impede them, and cannot cast spells or use magical items while enthralled. A successful save grants immunity for the encounter; the charm breaks only when every singing harpy is dead.
- **Magical Resilience**: +2 bonus to all saving throws.

## Hobgoblin
*Disciplined, militaristic goblinoids. Larger and better organized than goblins.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 1+1 (6hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d8 or by weapon), **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 8 (10 near their king), **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 15, **NA** 1d6 (4d6), **TT** D

- **Royal Retinue**: A lair holds a 5 HD king (22 hp, +2 damage) and 1d4 bodyguards (4 HD).

## Kobold
*Tiny reptilian humanoids, servile and trap-obsessed.*

**AC** 12, **HD** ½ (2hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d4 or by weapon −1), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 6 (8 near their chieftain), **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 5, **NA** 4d4 (6d10), **TT** P (J)

- **Ambushers**: Prefer to strike from surprise and from prepared traps.
- **Infravision**: 90'.
- **Gnome Enmity**: Will attack gnomes on sight.
- **Chieftain's Guard**: A lair holds a 2 HD chieftain (9 hp) and 1d6 bodyguards (1+1 HD, 6 hp each).

## Lizard Man
*Scaled humanoids from swamps and coasts, alien in thought and custom.*

**AC** 14, **HD** 2+1 (10hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6+1 or by weapon +1), **MV** 60' (20') (Swim 120' (40')), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 25, **NA** 2d4 (6d6), **TT** D

- **Preferred Arms**: Typically wield spears or heavy clubs.
- **Cannibal Raiders**: Kidnap humans and demihumans, prizing their flesh above other foods.

## Ogre
*Massive, dull-witted humanoids, 8–10 feet tall and incredibly strong.*

**AC** 14, **HD** 4+1 (19hp), **AB** +3, **Att** 1 × club (1d10), **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 10, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 125, **NA** 1d6 (2d6), **TT** C + 1,000 gp

- **Loot Sack**: Away from its lair an ogre carries a hide sack holding 1d6 × 100 gp.
- **Neanderthal Enmity**: Will attack Neanderthals on sight.

## Orc
*Brutish humanoids, raiders and warriors.*

**AC** 13, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 6 (8 with a leader), **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 2d4 (1d6 × 10), **TT** D

- **Daylight Weakness**: −1 to attack rolls in direct sunlight.
- **Cowardly**: Avoid larger or tougher foes unless their leaders compel them forward.
- **Leader**: Each band has a leader (8 hp) who deals +1 damage.
- **Chieftain**: A tribe is ruled by a 4 HD chieftain (15 hp, +2 damage).
- **Allied Brutes**: Per 20 orcs, there is a 1-in-6 chance an ogre marches with them and a 1-in-10 chance a troll dwells in the lair.
- **Tribal Rivalry**: Rival tribes fight each other unless held back by their leaders.

## Shadow
*Undead born from violent death, hungry for the life they've lost.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 2+2* (11hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × touch (1d4 + Strength Drain), **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 35, **NA** 1d8 (1d12), **TT** F

- **Stealthy**: Surprises on a 1–5 in 6.
- **Strength Drain**: Each touch drains 1 point of Strength, recovering after 8 turns. A victim reduced to Strength 0 dies and rises as a shadow.
- **Mundane-Proof**: Only magical weapons can wound it.
- **Mind-Magic Immunity**: Unaffected by charm or sleep effects.

## Skeleton
*Reanimated bones bound to a necromancer's will.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 1 (4hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d6 or by weapon), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 10, **NA** 3d4 (3d10), **TT** —

- **Silent**: Makes no sound until it strikes.
- **Undead**: Immune to poison and to effects that target only living creatures. Immune to charm, hold, and sleep.

## Stirge
*Vulture-sized blood-drinkers with a needle-like beak.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 1* (5hp), **AB** +0, **Att** 1 × beak (1d3 + Blood Drain), **MV** 30' (10') (Fly 180' (60')), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 9, **AL** Neutral, **XP** 13, **NA** 1d10 (3d12), **TT** L

- **Dive Attack**: +2 to the first attack roll when swooping in.
- **Blood Drain**: Once attached, automatically drains 1d3 hp per round. The stirge releases only when it or its victim dies.

## Troll
*A loathsome, rubbery giant whose torn flesh knits itself back together.*

**AC** 15, **HD** 6+3* (30hp), **AB** +5, **Att** 2 × talon (1d6) + 1 × bite (1d10), **MV** 120' (40'), **SV** D5 W4 P5 B4 S3, **ML** 10 (8 vs. fire or acid), **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 650, **NA** 1d8 (1d8), **TT** D

- **Regeneration**: Three rounds after taking its first wound, the troll heals 3 hp per round and can reattach severed limbs.
- **Reanimation**: If reduced to 0 hp by any means other than fire or acid, the troll rises again after 2d6 rounds with full hp.
- **Fire & Acid Weakness**: Damage from fire or acid cannot be regenerated and is the only way to slay the troll permanently.
- **Pyrophobia**: Morale drops to 8 the moment fire or acid is brought to bear.

## Wight
*A shrunken, white-eyed corpse that drains the life from the living.*

**AC** 14, **HD** 3* (14hp), **AB** +2, **Att** 1 × touch (Level Drain), **MV** 90' (30'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 50, **NA** 1d6 (1d8), **TT** B

- **Undead**: Silent until it attacks. Immune to poison and to charm, hold, and sleep.
- **Silver or Magic**: Untouched by ordinary weapons; only silver or magical arms can wound it.
- **Level Drain**: Each touch permanently strips one experience level (along with the HD, hp, and abilities of that level). A victim drained of all levels rises as a wight under the original's control within 1d4 days.

## Wolf
*Pack-hunting predators, swift and cooperative.*

**AC** 12, **HD** 2+2 (11hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × bite (1d6), **MV** 180' (60'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 6 (8 in packs of 4+), **AL** Neutral, **XP** 25, **NA** 2d6 (3d6), **TT** —

- **Pack Courage**: A pack of four or more fights at morale 8. The bonus is lost the moment the pack is reduced below half its starting count.
- **Trainable Young**: Cubs taken alive may be raised as guard or hunting animals, though it is difficult.

## Zombie
*Shambling corpses with no purpose but to attack the living.*

**AC** 11, **HD** 2 (9hp), **AB** +1, **Att** 1 × weapon (1d8 or by weapon), **MV** 60' (20'), **SV** D3 W2 P3 B2 S1, **ML** 12, **AL** Chaotic, **XP** 20, **NA** 2d4 (4d6), **TT** —

- **Always Last**: Loses initiative automatically; never rolls.
- **Undead**: Silent before they strike. Immune to poison and to charm, hold, and sleep.
