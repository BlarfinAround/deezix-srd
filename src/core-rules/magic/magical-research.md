# Magical Research

Spellcasters of sufficient experience can create permanent magical items and new spells through **magical research** — long periods of study, experimentation, and resource expenditure conducted in a suitable laboratory or sanctified space.

## Magical Research Failure

The failure roll below applies to every sub-procedure on this page (Scroll Creation, Holy Water, Potion Brewing, Spell Creation).

::: {.callout-important title="Magical Research Failure"}
Roll 1d6 when the research completes. On a **1**, the research fails and all time and money invested are lost.
:::

## Scroll Creation

Any spellcaster capable of magical research may attempt to **inscribe a spell scroll** — a single-use written spell that any appropriate user can cast without expending their own spell resources.

::: {.callout-important title="Scroll Creation"}

- **Time**: 1 week per spell level.
- **Cost**: 500 gp per spell level (rare inks, prepared parchment, binding materials).
- **Requirement**: The caster must know and be able to cast the spell being inscribed.
- **Result**: One scroll containing one casting of the spell.
:::

A Magic-User may inscribe arcane scrolls; a Cleric, divine scrolls.

## Holy Water and Sacred Oils (Clerics)

Clerics at level 3+ may attempt to create **holy water** and **blessed oils** — materials that harm undead and supernatural evil.

::: {.callout-important title="Holy Water"}

- **Time**: 1 day.
- **Cost**: 25 gp (blessed salts, consecrated vessel, ritual components).
- **Result**: One vial of holy water. When thrown, deals 1d8 damage to undead on a hit (treat as a ranged attack roll); undead without material form (such as specters) are repelled.
:::

**Blessed oil** can be applied to a weapon to make it function as a silver weapon for one combat encounter (25 gp, 1 day to create).

## Potion Brewing

Spellcasters at level 3+ may attempt to brew potions — single-use liquid forms of spell effects.

::: {.callout-important title="Potion Brewing"}

- **Time**: 1 week per spell level of the effect.
- **Cost**: 500 gp per spell level (alchemical reagents, containers, rare components specific to the effect).
- **Requirement**: The caster must know and be able to cast the spell being brewed. The effect must be appropriate to a potion (self-targeted, not area of effect, not requiring a target to be present).
:::

Potions take effect when consumed. Potion effects mirror the spell, using the minimum possible caster level and lasting 1d6+6 turns.

**Clerics** may brew **potions of healing only** — single-use vials produced from *Cure Light Wounds* under the rules above (1 week, 500 gp). Other divine effects are not bottled.

## Spell Research

Magic-Users at level 3+ may research spells: inventing a new effect, or adding a spell from the Arcane Spell list to their spellbook outside of leveling up.

::: {.callout-important title="Spell Creation"}

- **Time**: 2 weeks per spell level.
- **Cost**: 1,000 gp per spell level.
- **Process**: The caster describes the desired spell and its effects. The referee reviews and decides if the spell can be created, what spell level it would be, and any additional considerations. Alternatively, the caster researches an existing spell from the Arcane Spell list.
:::

### Copying a Spell

If a Magic-User is in possession of a scroll or spellbook containing the spell they wish to research, the time and cost to research the spell is reduced to **1 day and 250 gp per spell level**. If a spell scroll is used in this way, it disintegrates after the Magical Research attempt is complete.
