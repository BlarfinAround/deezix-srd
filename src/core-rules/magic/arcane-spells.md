# Arcane Spells

## 1st-Level Arcane Spells

### Charm Person
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Special

One humanoid (or human-like creature of 4+1 HD or less) makes a **Spells saving throw** or comes to regard the caster as a trusted friend. The charmed target obeys reasonable commands phrased in a language they understand, but refuses orders that contradict their nature or that would be suicidal. The target rolls a new save to break free at intervals set by their Intelligence: monthly (INT 3–8), weekly (INT 9–12), or daily (INT 13–18). Undead are immune.

### Detect Magic
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 2 turns

Magical items, places, and lingering enchantments within range glow faintly to the caster's sight. Reveals presence and rough intensity only — not the specific magic at work.

### Floating Disc
**Range**: 6' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Conjures an invisible, shield-shaped disc about 3' across that hovers at the caster's waist and trails them as they move. It carries up to 500 pounds of goods. When the duration ends, anything resting on the disc drops to the ground.

### Hold Portal
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: 2d6 turns

Magically seals a door, gate, window, or similar closure. The portal will not open by normal means for the duration. A *Knock* spell breaks it immediately, as does a creature with at least 3 HD more than the caster forcing it open over a single round.

### Light
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 turns + 1 turn/level

Creates a sphere of light (15' radius, equivalent to a torch but less than daylight) at a chosen point or on a movable object. May instead be aimed at a creature's eyes: target makes a **Spells saving throw** or is blinded for the duration. Also cancels an existing *Darkness*. *Reversed*: *Darkness* — a 15' radius of magical blackness that blocks normal sight (infravision still works), with the same blinding/cancellation options.

### Magic Missile
**Range**: 150' | **Duration**: 1 turn

A bolt of force unerringly strikes any visible target within range for **1d6+1 damage** — no attack roll, no saving throw. The caster fires one missile, plus one more for every two caster levels beyond 1st (two at level 3, three at level 5, and so on), to a maximum of five. Multiple missiles may be directed at the same or different targets.

### Protection from Evil
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 6 turns

The caster gains **+1 to all saving throws** against creatures of opposing alignment, and those creatures suffer **−1 to attack rolls** against the caster. Enchanted, conjured, or summoned creatures cannot make melee attacks against the caster at all (ranged attacks still function); this melee ward drops if the caster initiates melee with such a creature, though the +1/−1 bonuses remain.

### Read Languages
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 2 turns

The caster can read any written language, code, or symbology — treasure maps, foreign tomes, cipher fragments. Does not grant the ability to speak the language or to read magical writing (use *Read Magic* for that).

### Read Magic
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 1 turn

The caster deciphers arcane writing — spell scrolls, runes, and the spellbooks of other magic-users. Once read with this spell, that particular writing is permanently understood by the caster without recasting. A scroll must be read in this way before it can be invoked.

### Shield
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 2 turns

An invisible field forms around the caster: **AC 2** against missile attacks and **AC 4** against all other attacks for the duration.

### Sleep
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 4d4 turns

Drops non-undead creatures into magical slumber with no saving throw. Affects up to **2d8 total Hit Dice** of creatures, each of 4+1 HD or fewer — lowest HD first. Sleeping creatures are helpless: a single bladed attack kills them outright. Rough handling wakes them.

### Ventriloquism
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 2 turns

The caster's voice appears to come from any object or creature within range — a statue, a beast, an empty chair.

## 2nd-Level Arcane Spells

### Continual Light
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Permanent

As *Light*, but lasting indefinitely and brighter — a 30' radius of steady illumination on a point or movable object. May instead be aimed at a creature's eyes (**Spells saving throw** to negate), or used to cancel an active *Continual Darkness*. *Reversed*: *Continual Darkness* — a 30' radius of permanent magical blackness that blocks both normal sight and infravision.

### Detect Evil
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 2 turns

Objects bearing evil enchantments and creatures harboring evil intent glow to the caster's sight. Does not read minds. The referee adjudicates what counts as "evil" — Chaotic alignment is not automatically evil, and mindless hazards (traps, poison) are not.

### Detect Invisible
**Range**: 10' per level | **Duration**: 6 turns

The caster sees invisible creatures and objects within range. Range scales with caster level (50' at level 5).

### ESP
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 12 turns

The caster concentrates for one turn in a chosen direction, then perceives the surface thoughts of any creature there. Multiple minds blur together; spending another turn refocusing isolates one. Blocked by even a thin sheet of lead, or by 2' or more of solid rock.

### Invisibility
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Until broken

One creature (and gear carried) becomes invisible. The effect ends the moment the subject attacks or casts a spell. Dropped items become visible. Cast on an object instead, the invisibility is permanent and unbreakable. Light sources remain invisible but the light they cast does not.

### Knock
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 1 round

Opens any closure — locked, barred, stuck, or magically held (including *Hold Portal* and *Wizard Lock*). Reveals and opens known secret doors, but does not find unknown ones.

### Levitate
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 6 turns + 1 turn/level

The caster rises or descends at up to 20' per round under full control. Horizontal motion requires pushing off solid surfaces. The caster may carry their normal load while suspended.

### Locate Object
**Range**: 60' + 10' per level | **Duration**: 2 turns

The caster senses the direction (not distance) to either the nearest object of a known general type (stair, altar, gemstone) or a specific object they personally know. Cannot locate creatures.

### Mirror Image
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 6 turns

**1d4** illusory duplicates of the caster appear and mimic them exactly. Each attack against the caster destroys one image (whether or not the attack would have hit) until none remain.

### Phantasmal Force
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Concentration

Creates a vivid visual illusion within a 20' cube. The illusion may be:

- An attacking monster (AC 9, vanishes if struck in combat).
- An apparent natural disaster — avalanche, collapsing ceiling — affecting all within (**Spells saving throw** negates).
- A static scene that dissolves the moment it is touched.

Illusory "damage" is not real: apparent death causes 1d4 turns of unconsciousness; apparent petrification, 1d4 turns of paralysis. The caster must concentrate to maintain the illusion; any other action ends it. The referee may grant bonus saves to viewers unfamiliar with what the illusion depicts.

### Web
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: 48 turns

Fills a 10' cube with thick, sticky strands. Creatures caught inside must break free: normal strength takes **2d4 turns**, magically enhanced strength **4 rounds**, giant strength **2 rounds**. Burning the webs destroys them in 2 rounds; creatures inside take **1d6 damage** as they burn away.

### Wizard Lock
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: Permanent

Magically seals a door, gate, or similar portal indefinitely. The caster passes freely; *Knock* opens it without dispelling the lock; and any magic-user 3 or more levels above the caster may pass through. None of these bypasses break the enchantment — it stays in place.

## 3rd-Level Arcane Spells

### Clairvoyance
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 12 turns

After concentrating for one turn, the caster sees through the eyes of any one living creature within range. The caster may continue using that creature's senses or switch to another with another turn of concentration. Blocked by thin lead or by 2' or more of solid rock.

### Dispel Magic
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Instant

Cancels ongoing magical effects within a 20' cube. Effects from lower-level casters are automatically removed. Effects from a higher-level caster resist with a **5% chance per level** by which the original caster exceeds the dispeller. Has no effect on magic items.

### Fireball
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Instant

Detonates as a 20' radius sphere at a point of the caster's choice, dealing **1d6 damage per caster level** to every creature in the area. **Spells saving throw** halves the damage.

### Fly
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: 1d6 turns + 1 turn/level

The subject flies at up to **120' per round** in any direction, including hovering motionless. The referee rolls the duration in secret; the player does not know exactly when the spell ends.

### Haste
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 3 turns

Affects up to **24 creatures** in a 30' radius. Their movement rate and number of attacks per round are both doubled. Does not accelerate spellcasting or magic-item activation.

### Hold Person
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 1 turn per level

Paralyzes humanoid targets that fail a **Spells saving throw**. Does not affect undead or human-like creatures over 4+1 HD. May be cast at a single target (who saves at **−2**), or at a group (1d4 random members are affected, saving normally).

### Infravision
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: 1 day

Grants the subject infravision out to 60' for the duration.

### Invisibility 10' Radius
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Until broken

The target creature and every other creature within 10' of them become invisible. The bubble moves with the central target. Anyone who steps outside the radius becomes visible; anyone entering after the casting is not made invisible. Any individual who attacks or casts a spell becomes visible (only for themselves).

### Lightning Bolt
**Range**: 180' | **Duration**: Instant

A bolt 60' long and 5' wide springs from the caster, dealing **1d6 damage per caster level** to everything in its path. **Spells saving throw** halves the damage. If the bolt strikes a solid barrier before traveling its full 60', it ricochets back toward the caster for the remaining length.

### Protection from Evil 10' Radius
**Range**: 10' around the caster | **Duration**: 12 turns

As *Protection from Evil*, but warding all allies within 10' of the caster. Protected allies gain **+1 to saves** vs. attacks/abilities of creatures of opposing alignment, and such creatures suffer **−1 to attack rolls** against them. Enchanted, conjured, or summoned creatures cannot melee anyone in the warded area; this melee ward drops if any protected ally initiates melee with such a creature, though the +1/−1 bonuses remain.

### Protection from Normal Missiles
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 12 turns

One creature is wholly immune to small, non-magical missiles (arrows, bolts, sling stones, thrown daggers) for the duration. Offers no protection against hurled boulders, siege weapons, or enchanted projectiles.

### Water Breathing
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 1 day

The subject breathes water freely for the duration without losing the ability to breathe air. Does not increase swimming speed.

## 4th-Level Arcane Spells

### Charm Monster
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Special

As *Charm Person*, but affects any one living creature, or a swarm of small foes (**3d6 creatures** of 3 HD or fewer). Targets make a **Spells saving throw** or treat the caster as a trusted ally, obeying reasonable commands. Each rolls a new save to break free at intervals set by Intelligence: monthly (INT 3–8), weekly (INT 9–12), or daily (INT 13–18).

### Confusion
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 12 rounds

Up to **3d6 creatures** in a 30'-radius area are befuddled. Creatures of 2 HD or fewer are affected automatically; tougher creatures make a **Spells saving throw** each round. On a failure, roll the creature's behaviour:

| 2d6  | Behaviour                       |
| ---- | ------------------------------- |
| 2–5  | Attacks the caster's side       |
| 6–8  | Stands baffled, taking no action |
| 9–12 | Attacks its own side            |

### Dimension Door
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: Instant

Instantly transports the caster or one touched creature up to **360'** to a location the caster can picture. Arrival at a known spot is unerring; a blind guess may place the subject within a solid surface.

### Growth of Plants
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Permanent

Vegetation across a 3,000-square-foot area swells into a dense, clinging thicket, impassable without cutting a path. *Reversed*: *Shrink Plants* — clears an equal area of overgrowth.

### Hallucinatory Terrain
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Until touched or dispelled

Masks or fabricates terrain across a wide area — a pit reads as level ground, a road as marsh, open field as forest. The illusion holds until a creature physically interacts with the disguised feature.

### Massmorph
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Permanent

Up to **100 human-sized creatures** appear as a stand of trees or shrubs. They may move slowly without breaking the disguise, and other creatures pass among them without noticing. The effect lasts until the caster dismisses it.

### Polymorph Others
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: Permanent

Transforms one creature into another form (**Spells saving throw** negates). The subject gains the new form's movement and natural attacks but keeps its own mind and Hit Points. An unwilling change persists until dispelled.

### Polymorph Self
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 6 turns + 1 turn/level

The caster takes the shape of any creature of roughly their own mass, gaining its movement and natural attacks but not its special abilities, spellcasting, or Hit Dice. The caster keeps their own mind and Hit Points.

### Wall of Fire
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: Concentration

Raises a sheet of flame up to 60' long and 20' high, or a ring 30' across. Creatures of 3 HD or fewer cannot pass. Any creature forced through takes **1d6 damage**, doubled against undead and cold-using creatures. The wall stands while the caster concentrates.

### Wall of Ice
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 12 turns

Forms a wall of ice 60' long and 20' high, or a hemisphere 30' across. It blocks sight and passage; creatures of 3 HD or fewer cannot break through. Larger creatures forcing their way through take **1d6 damage**, doubled for fire-using creatures.

### Wizard Eye
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Conjures an invisible sensor the caster sees through. It drifts up to **120' per turn**, ranging out to 240' from the caster.

### Remove Curse
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: Instant

Lifts one curse from a creature, or frees them from a cursed item. *Reversed*: *Curse* — target makes a **Spells saving throw** or suffers a penalty the caster defines, up to **−2 to saves**, **−4 to attack rolls**, or a **50% reduction** to one ability score.

## 5th-Level Arcane Spells

### Animate Dead
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: Permanent

Raises skeletons or zombies from nearby corpses — **1 Hit Die of undead per caster level**. They obey simple commands until destroyed. Casting may carry alignment or faith consequences (referee's discretion).

### Cloudkill
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 6 turns

A roiling cloud of lethal vapour, 30' across, drifts away from the caster at **60' per round** and sinks into low ground. Creatures of fewer than 5 HD caught inside must make a **Death** save each round or die; weaker creatures flee before it.

### Conjure Elemental
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Permanent

Summons a **16 HD elemental** (air, earth, fire, or water) to serve the caster. Control demands unbroken concentration; if it lapses, the elemental turns on the caster. Only one elemental of each type may be summoned per day.

### Contact Other Plane
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: Special

The caster's mind reaches a distant plane to ask yes-or-no questions of an alien intelligence. More distant planes give truer answers but greater risk: on a failed **Spells saving throw**, the caster is left feebleminded for 1d6 weeks.

### Feeblemind
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Permanent

Targets one arcane spellcaster, who makes a **Spells saving throw at −4** or has their intellect shattered — unable to cast spells or communicate until restored by *Dispel Magic* or *Remove Curse*. Non-casters are unaffected.

### Hold Monster
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 turns + 1 turn per level

As *Hold Person*, but affects any creature. Cast at a single target (who saves at **−2**) or a group (1d4 affected, saving normally). **Spells saving throw** negates. Undead are immune.

### Magic Jar
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: Special

The caster seals their soul in a gem, then attempts to seize a body within 120' (**Spells saving throw** resists). While possessing the host, the caster uses its body and their own mind. If the host dies, the caster returns to the jar; if the empty jar is destroyed, the caster is lost.

### Pass-Wall
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 3 turns

Bores a passage 10' deep through solid rock or earth, wide enough to walk through. The passage seals when the spell ends, trapping anyone still inside.

### Telekinesis
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 rounds

The caster moves objects or creatures by will alone — up to **20 pounds per caster level** — at 20' per round in any direction. A creature resists with a **Spells saving throw**.

### Teleport
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: Instant

Instantly transports the caster, or one creature they touch, to any location the caster knows well. Hazy knowledge risks error: the referee may rule arrival high above the spot or fatally within a surface.

### Transmute Rock to Mud
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 3d6 days

Turns up to 3,000 square feet of rock or stone, to a depth of 10', into thick mud. Creatures in the area sink and are mired, crawling at best until they struggle free. *Reversed*: *Mud to Rock*.

### Wall of Stone
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: Permanent

Creates a wall of solid stone of up to 1,000 cubic feet, anchored to existing surfaces. It can seal a passage or bridge a gap, and lasts until broken by force or *Dispel Magic*.

## 6th-Level Arcane Spells

### Anti-Magic Shell
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 12 turns

An invisible barrier surrounds the caster, blocking all spells and magical effects from crossing in either direction. The caster cannot cast outward through it, and enchanted or conjured creatures cannot enter.

### Death Spell
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: Instant

Slays outright **4d8 Hit Dice** of creatures of 7 HD or fewer within a 60' area, with no saving throw. Stronger creatures and undead are unaffected.

### Disintegrate
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: Instant

Reduces one creature or a single object — up to the size of a ship — to dust (**Spells saving throw** negates). A disintegrated creature leaves nothing to raise. Magic items and force effects resist.

### Geas
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: Until fulfilled

Compels one creature (**Spells saving throw** negates) to undertake or avoid a task the caster sets. While the geas is defied, the subject sickens under a worsening affliction set by the referee, and may eventually die. *Reversed*: *Remove Geas*.

### Invisible Stalker
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: Until task complete

Summons an invisible elemental hunter to perform one task — track a quarry, guard a place, fetch an object. It pursues tirelessly until the task is done, then departs.

### Lower Water
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 10 turns

Lowers the level of water or similar liquid across a 10,000-square-foot area by half — fording a river, stranding a ship. *Reversed*: *Raise Water*.

### Move Earth
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Reshapes the landscape over a wide area — collapsing hills, raising banks, shifting earthworks — drifting at 60' per turn. Affects soil and earth, not worked stone.

### Part Water
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Parts a body of water to bare the bed beneath, opening a passage 10' wide and up to 120' long.

### Projected Image
**Range**: 240' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Projects an illusory double of the caster up to range. The caster's spells appear to issue from the image, drawing attacks toward it. Any hit on the image destroys it.

### Reincarnation
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: Instant

Returns a dead creature to life in a new body, its form determined by the referee — possibly a different kind of creature entirely.

### Stone to Flesh
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Permanent

Restores a petrified creature to living flesh. *Reversed*: *Flesh to Stone* — a creature within range makes a **Spells saving throw** or is turned to stone.

### Wall of Iron
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: Permanent

Conjures a wall of solid iron up to 500 square feet and several inches thick, anchored or free-standing. If created off-balance, it can be toppled onto creatures below.
