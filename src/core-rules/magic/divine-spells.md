# Divine Spells

## 1st-Level Divine Spells

### Cure Light Wounds
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: Instant

Heals **1d6+1 hit points** on a touched creature (not exceeding their maximum) and removes paralysis. *Reversed*: *Cause Light Wounds* — inflicts **1d6+1 damage** on a touched creature. In combat, a melee attack roll is required to deliver the touch.

### Detect Evil
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Objects bearing evil enchantments and creatures harboring evil intent glow to the caster's sight. Does not read minds. The referee adjudicates what counts as "evil" — Chaotic alignment is not automatically evil, and mindless hazards are not.

### Detect Magic
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 2 turns

Magical items, places, and lingering enchantments within range glow faintly to the caster's sight. Reveals presence and rough intensity only.

### Light
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 12 turns

Creates a sphere of light (15' radius, equivalent to a torch) at a chosen point or on a movable object. May instead be aimed at a creature's eyes: target makes a **Spells saving throw** or is blinded for the duration. Also cancels an existing *Darkness*. *Reversed*: *Darkness* — a 15' radius of magical blackness that blocks normal sight (infravision still works), with the same blinding/cancellation options.

### Protection from Evil
**Range**: The caster | **Duration**: 12 turns

The caster gains **+1 to all saving throws** against creatures of opposing alignment, and those creatures suffer **−1 to attack rolls** against the caster. Enchanted, conjured, or summoned creatures cannot make melee attacks against the caster at all (ranged still functions); this melee ward drops if the caster initiates melee with such a creature, though the +1/−1 bonuses remain.

### Purify Food and Water
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: Permanent

Removes poison, rot, or contamination from food or water. One casting purifies up to **6 quarts** of drink, one ration, or unpreserved food enough for **12 people**.

### Remove Fear
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: 2 turns

Calms a touched creature and dispels existing fear. Against magically induced fear, the subject re-rolls their save with **+1 per caster level**. *Reversed*: *Cause Fear* — a target within 120' makes a **Spells saving throw** or flees in terror for the duration.

### Resist Cold
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 6 turns

All creatures within range take no damage from non-magical cold, gain **+2 to saving throws** against cold-based attacks (spells, breath weapons, magical effects), and reduce cold damage by **1 per die rolled** (each die still inflicts at least 1).

## 2nd-Level Divine Spells

### Bless
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 6 turns

Allies in a 20' square who have not yet entered melee gain **+1 to attack rolls**, **+1 to damage rolls**, and **+1 to morale**. The spell may also serve as part of consecration or purification rites at the referee's discretion. *Reversed*: *Blight* — enemies in a 20' square suffer **−1 to morale, attack rolls, and damage rolls** (**Spells saving throw** negates).

### Find Traps
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 2 turns

All traps within range — magical or mechanical — glow with a faint blue light visible only to the caster. Reveals their location but not their nature or how to disarm them.

### Hold Person
**Range**: 180' | **Duration**: 9 turns

Paralyzes humanoid targets that fail a **Spells saving throw**. Does not affect undead or human-like creatures over 4+1 HD. May be cast at a single target (who saves at **−2**), or at a group (1d4 random members are affected, saving normally).

### Know Alignment
**Range**: 10' | **Duration**: 1 round

Reveals the alignment of one creature, object, or place within range. Most ordinary objects have no alignment, but magic items and consecrated (or desecrated) sites do.

### Resist Fire
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 2 turns

One creature takes no damage from normal heat or fire, gains **+2 to saving throws** against fire-based attacks (spells, breath weapons, magical effects), and reduces fire damage by **1 per die rolled** (each die still inflicts at least 1).

### Silence, 15' Radius
**Range**: 180' | **Duration**: 12 turns

A 15' radius zone falls utterly silent: no speech and no spellcasting are possible inside it. Sounds from outside can still be heard within. May instead be cast on a creature, which makes a **Spells saving throw**. On a failed save, the zone moves with the target; on a success, the zone fixes in place where the target stood, and the target may simply step out of it.

### Snake Charm
**Range**: 60' | **Duration**: 1d4+1 rounds or turns

Pacifies snakes — they rear and sway but do not attack. Affects up to **1 HD of snake per caster level**. Duration is **1d4+1 rounds** if the snakes were already in combat, otherwise **1d4+1 turns**.

### Speak with Animals
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 6 turns

The caster chooses one species of normal or giant animal within range and can converse with that type for the duration. Does not work on intelligent animals or fantastical monsters. Friendly animals may agree to perform services within their understanding and ability.

## 3rd-Level Divine Spells

### Continual Light
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: Permanent

As *Light*, but lasting indefinitely and as bright as daylight — a 30' radius of steady illumination on a point or movable object. Affects creatures penalized by sunlight. May instead be aimed at a creature's eyes (**Spells saving throw** to negate), or used to cancel an active *Continual Darkness*. *Reversed*: *Continual Darkness* — a 30' radius of permanent magical blackness that blocks both normal sight and infravision; mundane light sources fail to illuminate it.

### Cure Disease
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: Instant

Cures any disease (including magical) on a single creature, and instantly destroys green slime. *Reversed*: *Cause Disease* — target makes a **Spells saving throw** or contracts a wasting illness. The afflicted suffers **−2 to attack rolls**, heals at half the normal rate, and cannot be magically healed. The disease kills in **2d12 days** if not removed by *Cure Disease*.

### Growth of Animal
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 12 turns

Doubles the size and strength of one normal or giant animal — doubling its attack damage and carrying capacity. Does not affect intelligent animals, magical creatures, or fantastical monsters.

### Locate Object
**Range**: 120' | **Duration**: 6 turns

The caster senses the direction (not distance) to either the nearest object of a known general type (stair, altar, gemstone) or a specific object they can clearly visualize. Cannot locate creatures.

### Remove Curse
**Range**: The caster or a creature touched | **Duration**: Instant

Removes one curse from a creature, and may allow them to be rid of a cursed item. *Reversed*: *Curse* — target makes a **Spells saving throw** or suffers a curse the caster defines, up to a maximum penalty of **−2 to saves**, **−4 to attack rolls**, or a **50% reduction** to one ability score. Multiple curses with different effects may stack on the same victim. The referee may rule that overpowered curses turn back on their caster.

### Striking
**Range**: 30' | **Duration**: 1 turn

Enchants one weapon to deal an extra **1d6 damage** per hit, and to count as magical for the purpose of striking creatures only harmed by magical weapons.
