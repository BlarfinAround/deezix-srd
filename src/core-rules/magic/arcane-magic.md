# Arcane Magic

Arcane magic is the [Magic-User](../character/classes/magic-user.md)'s tradition: studied, memorized, and recorded in a spellbook.

## The Spellbook

The spellbook contains every arcane spell the Magic-User knows. Without it, they cannot prepare new spells.

- **Starting spellbook**: Two 1st-level spells. Provided free at character creation.
- **Acquiring spells**: a Magic-User acquires new spells via [Spell Research](../magic/magical-research.md#spell-research).

## Preparing Spells

After 8 hours of rest, the Magic-User spends 1 hour studying their spellbook and fills their available **spell slots**. The same spell may be prepared into multiple slots.

## Reversed Spells

Some arcane spells (e.g., *Light*/*Darkness*) have a reversed form noted in their entry. A Magic-User treats the reversed form as a **separate spell**: it must be researched, recorded in the spellbook, and prepared into its own slot.

## Scroll Use

Before an arcane scroll can be cast, the Magic-User must first read it — by casting *Read Magic* or with a successful **Arcane Lore** roll. General [Scroll Use](magic-overview.md#scroll-use) rules apply.
