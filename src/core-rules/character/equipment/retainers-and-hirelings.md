# Retainers and Hirelings

Adventuring parties often want help — someone to hold the torches, watch the horses, or fight alongside them. **Retainers** are part of the party; **hirelings** are paid specialists hired for specific tasks.

## Hirelings

Hirelings perform specific jobs but do not adventure in the dungeon proper. They have no levels and will not fight monsters.

| Hireling             | Daily Wage | Notes                                   |
| -------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Porter / torchbearer | 1 sp       | Carries gear or light; stays at edges   |
| Guide (local)        | 5 sp       | Knows the area; helps with navigation   |
| Physician            | 2 gp       | Tends wounds without magic; aids rest   |
| Sage / scholar       | 50+ gp     | Expert knowledge; won't risk themselves |

Hirelings flee if combat reaches their position. They expect to be paid regardless of success.

## Retainers

Retainers are adventuring companions — they enter the dungeon, take risks, and earn a share of treasure. They develop loyalty and can grow over time.

### Finding Retainers

Recruited in towns via word of mouth, posted notices, or taverns. Typical offer: half a share of treasure + 1d6 gp/day wage + equipment.

### Limits and Loyalty

A character's CHA determines the maximum retainers they can employ and the base **[Loyalty](../../referee/reactions-morale-loyalty.md#loyalty)** of those retainers.

| CHA   | Max Retainers | Base Loyalty |
| ----- | ------------- | ------------ |
| 3     | 1             | 4            |
| 4–5   | 2             | 5            |
| 6–8   | 3             | 6            |
| 9–12  | 4             | 7            |
| 13–15 | 5             | 8            |
| 16–17 | 6             | 9            |
| 18    | 7             | 10           |

### Retainer Statistics

A retainer begins as a 0-level human or with a class and level no greater than the hiring character.

**0-level retainer:** AC 12 (leather) · HP 1d6 · Attack +0, damage 1d6 (or as weapon) · Saves all 2-in-6.

Retainers earn XP at half a share and may advance in level.
