**Weapon Properties**

- **Blunt**: May be used by clerics.
- **Mounted only**: ineffective unless the character is mounted.
- **Range**: at *Near* range, attacks roll normally. At *Far* range, attacks suffer **−2**. Beyond far range, attacks are impossible.
- **Silver**: Effective against certain supernatural creatures.
- **Throwable**: May be used as a *ranged weapon*.
- **Two Handed**: The character cannot use a shield.

## Armor Class

**Armor Class (AC)** measures how hard a character is to hit in combat. An attack hits if the attacker's roll equals or exceeds the target's AC.

Worn armor sets the character's base AC. The character's **DEX modifier** is applied to AC.

| Armor             | AC  | Cost   |
| ----------------- | --- | ------ |
| Unarmored         | 10  | —      |
| Leather armor     | 12  | 20 gp  |
| ***Chain mail***  | 14  | 60 gp  |
| ***Plate armor*** | 16  | 300 gp |
| Shield            | +1  | 10 gp  |

### Shields

A shield is held in one hand and adds **+1 AC**. A shield cannot be used with two-handed weapons.


*Items in italic are bundleable.* ***Items in bold italic are oversized.*** Worn armor counts as 0 items. See [Encumbrance](../encumbrance.md).
