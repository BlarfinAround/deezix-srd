# Character Creation
## Step 1 — Roll Ability Scores

Roll **3d6 six times in order**: STR, INT, WIS, DEX, CON, CHA. Record each result.

Consult the [Ability Scores](ability-scores.md) page for modifiers and what each score affects.

## Step 2 — Choose a Class

Choose one of the four core classes:

| Class | Key Ability | Armor | Magic |
|-------|------------|-------|-------|
| [Fighter](classes/fighter.md) | STR | Any | None |
| [Cleric](classes/cleric.md) | WIS | Leather or chain | Divine |
| [Magic-User](classes/magic-user.md) | INT | None | Arcane |
| [Thief](classes/thief.md) | DEX | Leather | None |

## Step 3 — Record Class Features

Copy the class's 1st-level values from the class page: Attack Bonus, Saving Throws, Special Abilities. Spellcasters also note their starting spell slots and prepared spells.

## Step 4 — Roll Hit Points

Roll the class's Hit Die (see [Hit Points and Death](../adventuring/hit-points-and-death.md)) and add the character's CON modifier. This is the starting HP.

**Minimum 1 HP**, regardless of modifiers.

## Step 5 — Record Skills

All characters begin with four **General Skills** at a **1-in-6 chance**:

- [Break](../adventuring/skills.md#break)
- [Languages](../adventuring/skills.md#languages)
- [Search/Listen](../adventuring/skills.md#search-listen)
- [Tumble](../adventuring/skills.md#tumble)

Apply the class's key ability modifier to its designated General Skill (see [Skills](../adventuring/skills.md)):

- **Fighter**: STR → Break
- **Cleric**: WIS → Search/Listen
- **Magic-User**: INT → Languages (plus Arcane Lore class skill at 2-in-6)
- **Thief**: DEX → Tumble (plus four class skills at 1-in-6 and 2 skill points to allocate)

See the class entry for any class skills.

## Step 6 — Buy Equipment

Roll **3d6 × 10 gp** for starting gold. See the class entry for recommended purchases, allowed weapons/armor, and the [Equipment](equipment/starting-wealth.md) section for the full item lists.

Magic-Users begin play with a free spellbook containing **two 1st-level spells** of the player's choice.

## Step 7 — Determine Armor Class

Worn armor sets the character's **AC** (10 if unarmored, up to 16 in plate). Add the DEX modifier, and +1 for a shield. See [Armor Class](equipment/weapons-and-armor.md#armor-class) for the table.

## Step 8 — Final Details

- **Name** appropriate to the setting.
- **Languages**: Native tongue + 1 language per INT bonus.
- **XP**: Begin at 0.
- **Alignment**: Law, Neutral, or Chaos.

## Character Sheet Summary

A completed character sheet lists:

- Name, Class, Level, Alignment, XP
- Ability scores and modifiers
- HP, AC, AB
- Saving throw values
- Skills
- Equipment
- **For casters**: spell slots and prepared spells

::: {.callout-note title="Example: Fighter"}
Marta Ironfist · Lawful Fighter · Level 1 (0 XP)

**STR** 15 (+1) · **INT** 9 · **WIS** 10 · **DEX** 12 · **CON** 14 (+1) · **CHA** 8 (−1)

**HP** 9 (d8+1) · **AC** 15 (chain + shield) · **AB** +1

**Attacks**: Sword (+2 attack, 1d8+1 damage) · Short bow (+1 attack, 1d6 damage, 50'/100')

**Death** 3 · **Wands** 2 · **Paralysis** 3 · **Breath** 2 · **Spells** 1

**Skills**: Break 2-in-6, Search/Listen 1-in-6, Languages 1-in-6, Tumble 1-in-6

**Languages**: Common

**Equipment**: Sword, Short bow, Arrows ×20, Shield, Chain Mail (worn), Rope, Torches ×6, Rations ×7, Tinderbox

**Gold**: 12 gp
:::
