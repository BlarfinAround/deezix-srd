# Cleric

The Cleric is a holy warrior skilled in combat and divine spell casting to protect allies and otherworldly foes.

## Class Summary

| Property             | Value                                       |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Hit Die**          | d6                                          |
| **Armor**            | Leather or chain, shields                   |
| **Weapons**          | Blunt only (maces, flails, hammers, staves) |
| **Magic**            | Divine (prepared spells; full class list)   |
| **Magical Research** | Scrolls, holy water, blessed oils, potions of healing (level 3+) |

## Skills

The Cleric's WIS modifier applies to [Search/Listen](../../adventuring/skills.md#search-listen).

### Class Skills

**Turn Undead**
The Cleric may channel holy power to repel or destroy undead.

- **Start**: 2-in-6 at level 1
- Improves by +1 at level 4

Once per encounter, the Cleric presents a holy symbol and rolls Turn Undead.

::: {.callout-important title="Turn Undead"}
**On success**: **2d6 HD of undead** within sight flee for 1d6 turns, drawn **lowest-HD first**.

**On a roll of 1**: the affected undead are **destroyed outright** instead of fleeing.

**On failure**: 1d6 undead with HD 1 or less within sight flee for 1d6 turns.

Undead with HD greater than **Cleric Level + 1** are unaffected by **Turn Undead**.
:::


## Casting

The Cleric **prepares** divine spells each morning. They know every spell on the [Divine Spell List](../../magic/divine-spells.md) for the spell levels they have unlocked, and choose which to prepare from that full list. The same spell may be prepared into multiple slots. Once cast, a prepared spell is expended until the next morning's preparation.

See [Divine Magic](../../magic/divine-magic.md).

## Class Abilities

### Magical Research (Level 3+)
At 3rd level, the Cleric may create divine scrolls and holy water. See [Magical Research](../../magic/magical-research.md).

## Starting Equipment (Suggested)

Mace, shield, leather or chain mail, holy symbol, torches ×6, rations ×7, tinderbox.
