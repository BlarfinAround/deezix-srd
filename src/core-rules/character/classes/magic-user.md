# Magic-User

The Magic-User is a scholar and researcher of arcane knowledge who trades direct combat abilities for a collection of powerful spells.

## Class Summary

| Property             | Value                                       |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Hit Die**          | d4                                          |
| **Armor**            | None                                        |
| **Weapons**          | Daggers, staffs, slings                     |
| **Magic**            | Arcane (spellbook; prepared spells)         |
| **Magical Research** | Scrolls, potions, spell creation (level 3+) |

## Skills

The Magic-User's INT modifier applies to [Languages](../../adventuring/skills.md#languages).

### Class Skills

**Arcane Lore**
Chance-based ability to detect magic, read magical writing, identify magic items, or recall arcane knowledge.

- **Start**: 2-in-6 at level 1
- Improves at levels 4, 9, and 14 (maximum 5-in-6)

Roll Arcane Lore to:

- **Detect Magic** as the *Detect Magic* spell (limited to 30' range and duration of 1 turn).
- **Read Magic** as the *Read Magic* spell (one attempt per magical text).
- **Identify** a magical item, learning its properties and activation method (one attempt per item; identification may not be reattempted until the Magic-User gains a level).
- Recall obscure **magical lore** or a magical creature's properties.

Detecting magic, reading magic, or identifying with **Arcane Lore** takes 1 turn.

## Casting

The Magic-User follows the [Arcane Magic](../../magic/arcane-magic.md) tradition: spells are prepared from the spellbook each morning, equal to available slots (see Advancement Table above).

## Class Abilities

### Magical Research (Level 3+)
At 3rd level, the Magic-User may create arcane scrolls, brew potions, and research new spells.

## Starting Equipment (Suggested)

Spellbook (free), staff, sling, ink and quill, torches ×6, rations ×7, tinderbox.
