# Thief

The Thief is a skill specialist — lightly armored but competent in combat, with a custom skill loadout improved every level.

## Skills

The Thief's DEX modifier applies to [Tumble](../../adventuring/skills.md#tumble).

The Thief begins with **four Class Skills**, each at a **1-in-6 chance**, plus **2 skill points** at character creation. Gain **2 additional skill points per level**. Points may be spent to improve any General or Class Skill by +1 per point (maximum 5-in-6 chance).

### Class Skills

**Climb**
Scaling sheer or difficult surfaces without gear — dungeon walls, cliffs, ship rigging, castle parapets.

- Roll when ascending a surface without handholds or assistance.
- Failure mid-climb may result in a fall (1d6 per 10' fallen).
- Ordinary climbing (ladders, knotted ropes, rough stone with clear handholds) does not require a roll.

**Tinker**
Picking locks, disarming mechanical traps, and working with intricate mechanisms.

- Requires [thieves' tools](../equipment/adventuring-gear.md).
- Roll when attempting to open a lock without the key or to safely neutralize a mechanical trap.
- Failure when disarming a trap may trigger it.
- Failing to pick a lock means the lock is beyond the thief's abilities and may not be attempted until they have gained a level.

**Stealth**
Moving without sound and concealing oneself from observers.

- Roll when attempting to move through an area without being seen or heard.
- In bright light or against actively searching opponents: −1 to target number.
- Stealth requires relative quiet, shadow, or objects to use as cover. It is impossible in full daylight and open terrain.

**Sleight of Hand**
Lifting objects from people undetected, palming items, or performing manual misdirection.

- Roll when picking a pocket, slipping an item into someone's bag, or concealing a manual action.
- Alert, suspicious, or prepared targets impose −1.

### Starting Skills (Example)
A 1st-level Thief begins with all four class skills at a 1-in-6 chance and 2 skill points to spend:

- Spend 1 on Stealth (→ 2-in-6)
- Spend 1 on Tinker (→ 2-in-6)

## Class Abilities

### Backstab

::: {.callout-important title="Backstab"}
A melee attack against a target who is unaware of the Thief (surprised, or attacked after a successful Stealth roll) is made at a **+4 attack bonus** and deals **bonus weapon dice** of damage, scaling with level.

| Thief Level | Bonus Damage |
|-------|-------------|
| 1–2 | +1 weapon die |
| 3–4 | +2 weapon dice |
| 5–8 | +3 weapon dice |
| 9–12 | +4 weapon dice |
| 13–15 | +5 weapon dice |

The bonus applies only to the first attack; the target is aware afterward.
:::
