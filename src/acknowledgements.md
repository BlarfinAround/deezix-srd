# Acknowledgements

Deezix sits within a long lineage of fantasy roleplaying systems, and owes a debt to the designers and communities whose work influenced and inspired it.

## Influential Games

- **Dungeons & Dragons (1974)** by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson
- **Advanced Dungeons & Dragons (1977)** by Gary Gygax
- **Dungeons & Dragons Basic/Expert (1981)** by Tom Moldvay and David "Zeb" Cook
- **OSRIC (2006)** by Stuart Marshall and Matt Finch
- **Basic Fantasy Role-Playing Game (2007)** by Chris Gonnerman
- **Lamentations of the Flame Princess (2013)** by James Edward Raggi IV
- **White Star (2015)** by James M. Spahn
- **Old-School Essentials (2019)** by Gavin Norman
- **Dolmenwood (2025)** by Gavin Norman
