# Expanding the Game

Deezix's core rules cover play through level 5 with the four base classes and a low-level bestiary. This section sketches how the chassis extends past level 5.

## Level 6 and Beyond
Characters typically advance through level 9, then take their place in the game world as lords, patriarchs, guild masters, or renowned sages (see **Domain Activities** below). Beyond level 9, progress continues at a slower pace with diminished benefits.

### Saving Throws
All saves improve by +1 at level 5 and again at level 10. Saves cap at a 5-in-6 chance:

| Level | Strong | Standard | Weak   |
| ----- | ------ | -------- | ------ |
| 5     | 4-in-6 | 3-in-6   | 2-in-6 |
| 10+   | 5-in-6 | 4-in-6   | 3-in-6 |

### Hit Points
Continue rolling the class Hit Die each level through level 9. Starting level 10+ add a flat +2 HP per level (CON modifier no longer applies).

### Specialist Skill Points
+2 skill points per level continues through level 9. Starting at level 10+, add +1 skill points every other level.

## Additional Spell Levels

Levels 4–6 provide the following benchmark effects:

| Level | Power | Examples |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 4th | Major transformation, large area, powerful ward | Wall of Fire, Confusion, Dimension Door |
| 5th | Devastating; permanent effects; powerful conjuration | Cloudkill, Teleport, Animate Dead |
| 6th | Legendary; reality-bending | Disintegrate, Geas, Stone to Flesh |

Design guidelines scale to higher levels. Higher-level spells should feel qualitatively different — not just "more damage" but "new categories of problem-solving."

## High-Level Campaign Activities

At level 9+, characters may begin pursuing **domain activities**:

- **Fighters** may establish a keep or stronghold, attracting followers.
- **Clerics** may found a temple or shrine, drawing faithful.
- **Magic-Users** may establish a tower with a library and laboratory.
- **Thieves** may found or join a guild.

## Extended Monster Tiers

Higher-level play requires stronger monsters. Example tier above basic play:

| HD | Threat Level | Examples |
|----|-------------|---------|
| 7–8 | High | Giants, hydras, powerful undead (vampires, liches) |
| 9–11 | Very High | Dragons (young), demon lords, powerful elementals |
| 12+ | Legendary | Ancient dragons, archdevils, demigods |
